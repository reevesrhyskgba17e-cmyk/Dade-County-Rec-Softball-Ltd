Who We Are

Community. Teamwork. Growth.

Dade County Rec Softball Ltd is a registered non-profit organization dedicated to fostering the love of softball in Trenton, GA.

Operating out of our facilities on Piney Road, we offer recreational and competitive leagues for youth and adults. Our mission is to provide affordable, organized sports programs that promote physical fitness, sportsmanship, and community engagement.

What We Offer

Our Leagues

Programs designed for every age group and skill level. Whether you're just learning to throw or a seasoned veteran, there's a spot for you on the diamond.

Youth Fastpitch

  • Ages: 6U, 8U, 10U, 12U & 14U
  • Season: Spring & Fall
  • Focus: Fundamentals, sportsmanship, and skill development.

Our core program focuses on teaching girls the fundamentals of fastpitch softball in a supportive, fun environment.

Adult Slowpitch

  • Divisions: Men's, Women's, Co-Ed
  • Season: Summer & Fall
  • Focus: Competition, community, and recreation.

Competitive and recreational divisions available. Get your team together and enjoy evenings under the lights.

Skills Clinics

  • Events: Pitching, Hitting, Fielding
  • Schedule: Pre-season & Winter
  • Instructors: Former college athletes & coaches.

Specialized training sessions designed to help players refine their mechanics and take their game to the next level.
